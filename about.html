What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01 — Safety

Safety First, Always

Every policy, feature, and decision starts with one question: does this make our members safer? We maintain strict verification, zero-tolerance harassment policies, and 24/7 moderation to ensure LadiesOwnly is a refuge, not another risk.

02 — Authenticity

Real Women, Real Stories

We reject the curated, performative culture of other platforms. LadiesOwnly is a place for honest conversations — about failure, ambition, motherhood, grief, joy, and everything in between. Authenticity is our culture.

03 — Inclusion

Belonging for All Women

LadiesOwnly is for every woman — regardless of age, race, background, industry, or life stage. We actively work to ensure our platform reflects the full, beautiful diversity of womanhood across the globe.

04 — Growth

Grow or Go Home

We are relentlessly committed to our members' growth — professional, personal, and spiritual. Every resource, event, and mentor match is chosen with one outcome in mind: helping you become who you're meant to be.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2020 — The Idea

Two Women, One Conversation

Co-founders Nadia and Priya met at a women-in-tech conference and realised they were looking for the same thing — a space online that felt as warm, honest, and empowering as that room. Six months later, they built it.

2021 — First 1,000 Members

The Community Found Its Voice

Word spread fast. Within six months of launch, LadiesOwnly had 1,000 verified members across 14 countries. Our first virtual summit drew 800 attendees. The need was undeniable.

2022 — Mentorship Programme

Structured Growth at Scale

We launched our formal mentorship matching programme, pairing 500 women in its first cohort. 90% of mentees reported significant career progression within 6 months. The model was proven.

2024 — 50,000 Members

A Global Movement

We crossed 50,000 members in 82 countries. We opened regional chapters in Lagos, Mumbai, London, São Paulo, and Singapore. LadiesOwnly is now a global force — and we're just getting started.
